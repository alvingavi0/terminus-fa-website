About Us

Learn more about Terminus Soccer Academy and our commitment to empowering kids and teenagers through sports and education.

Our Mission

To use sports, education and leadership as tools for creating disciplined and hardworking youths who can help solve problems faced in their communities and become role models to each other.

Our Vision

Empower 1000 kids and teenagers by 2030 through talent development and education so as to reduce poverty levels in slums.

Our Values

Integrity

We believe in handing kids and teenagers and youths opportunities to get skills and knowledge to earn an honest living.

Hard Work

We believe that talent alone is not enough. In everything we teach the kids and teenagers, we ensure that they keep giving their best and some more on top.

Cooperation

Teamwork is key to ensuring that the students within our programs develop empathy, love and trust for each other.

Equality

We believe in handing out equal opportunities to all kids and teenagers irrespective of gender, physical state, religious affiliation or tribe.

Background

Terminus Soccer Academy is a non-profit organization founded in the heart of Kibera Slums with the aim to provide young children living in slum communities with the opportunity to participate in organized football (soccer) activities while at the same time enabling them to pursue academic excellence.
