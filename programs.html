Our Programs

Learn more about the Football and Education Programs at Terminus Soccer Academy.

Football Program

This is the main program at Terminus Soccer Academy. Our goal is to ensure that most of the kids and teenagers within our programs are able to grow and develop their footballing skills to successfully play the game professionally.

Benefits for kids and teenagers:

  • Daily 2-hour training sessions in the evenings after classes during the academic season.
  • Double training sessions (Morning and evening) during the holiday seasons.
  • Friendly matches with other teams to gain competitive experience.
  • Weekly video classes to study various aspects of football.
  • Up to 5 Tournaments annually (both local and international) for more exposure and experience.
  • Mentorship sessions from accomplished retired and current players in local and international professional leagues.
  • Sourcing and securing placement into secondary schools and universities on sports scholarships
  • Trial and scouting opportunities with local and foreign professional clubs.

Methodology:

Selection and onboarding of kids and teenagers to the programs

We will find the most needy and talented kids and teenagers who we can work with. We will engage with parents/guardians and community leaders to seek permission and blessings to work with their kids and teenagers.

Recruitment of coaches and volunteers

We will recruit and train local coaches and volunteers to lead the football activities. Coaches will be recruited for all categories.(under 11, under 13, Under 15, under 18)

Procurement of necessary equipment

We will provide each participating community with a set of footballs, cones, and other necessary equipment.

Organizing and implementing football sessions

The kids and teenagers will undergo football sessions under three main categories. Training sessions- normal evening sessions during academic period and morning and evening sessions during holidays. Select tournaments and friendly matches for building confidence, experience and match fitness. This will also provide exposure of the kids and teenagers to scouts and professional teams. Football classes-Video and theory classes for the players to understand and analyze various football tactics, systems and game situations.

Post-program support

This phase will involve facilitating the transition of the kids and teenagers from Terminus Soccer Academy to either professional or advanced academic levels. This will be done in the following ways: Terminus Soccer Academy will source for scholarship opportunities at secondary schools and universities. We will help the kids and teenagers apply for these scholarships. We will also build relationships with the academic institutions with the aim of forming partnerships that can facilitate awarding of a few slots of scholarships to our kids and teenagers. We will also work on securing trials opportunities for the kids and teenagers with professional football clubs upon reaching age 16 and above.

Evaluation of project impact

We will conduct surveys and interviews with participating children, their parents/guardians, and coaches and partners to assess the impact of the project on fitness levels, teamwork skills, and personal development of the kids and teenagers as well as community development and impact of the program.

Education Program

The education program will be key to ensuring that the kids and teenagers and youths within our programs safeguard their futures. Even as we aim to maximize their chances of becoming professional

The education program will be key to ensuring that the kids and teenagers and youths within our programs safeguard their futures. Even as we aim to maximize their chances of becoming professional footballers, we understand the importance of a solid education.

Objectives and activities for the education program:

Mentorship program

We will pair each child and teenager with a mentor who will provide guidance and support in their academic pursuits. The mentors will be teachers, community leaders and role models.

Academic support

We will provide tutoring and homework assistance to children and teenagers who are struggling in school. We will also offer workshops on study skills and time management.

Scholarship program

We will provide scholarships to children and teenagers who are excelling academically but are unable to afford school fees.

Life skills training

We will offer workshops on life skills such as communication, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Career guidance

We will provide career guidance to teenagers to help them make informed decisions about their future.

Community library

We will establish and equip a community library with relevant reading materials to support learning.
